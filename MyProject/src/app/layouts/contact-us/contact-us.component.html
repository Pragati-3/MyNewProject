“Believe nothing, O monks, merely because you have been told it or because is it traditional, or because you yourselves have imagined it.
Do not believe what your teacher tells you merely out of respect for the teacher. But, whatsoever, after due examination and analysis, you find to be conducive to the good, the benefit,
the welfare of all beings–that doctrine believe and cling to, and take it as your guide.”
